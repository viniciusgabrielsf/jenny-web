## Design Context

### Users

Young adults using Jenny on both desktop and mobile. They come to manage money in a practical, everyday context: tracking payments and transactions, organizing groups and shared expenses, and updating personal/profile data.

### Brand Personality

Clean, cheerful, and weightless. The UI should feel calm, easy to approach, and lightly optimistic rather than heavy, corporate, or overly serious.

### Aesthetic Direction

A distinctive but restrained finance experience that avoids generic Apple-style polish and Material UI sameness. The visual language should feel airy, friendly, and intentionally composed, with clear hierarchy, warm cohesion, and enough personality to feel memorable without becoming loud.

### Design Principles

- Keep the interface light in tone and visually uncluttered, even when the data is dense.
- Make core actions obvious and fast on both desktop and mobile.
- Use a cohesive palette and typography system that feels friendly but not childish.
- Favor custom composition and spacing over default component-library patterns.
- Preserve the current brand assets and existing theme structure, but refine them into a more distinctive system.
